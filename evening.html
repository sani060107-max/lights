🌙 Evening Duas

End your day with peace and protection.

Dua for the Evening

اللّهـمّ بك أمسينا وبك أصبحنا، وبك نحيا وبك نموت وإليك المصير

“O Allah, by You we enter the evening and by You we enter the morning, by You we live and by You we die, and to You is the final return.”

Dua for Forgiveness

اللَّهُمَّ إِنِّي أَمْسَيْتُ أُشْهِدُكَ، وَأُشْهِدُ حَمَلَةَ عَرْشِكَ...

“O Allah, I have entered the evening calling You, the bearers of Your Throne, Your angels, and all creation to witness that You are Allah, none has the right to be worshiped except You...”

“Glorify your Lord before the rising of the sun and before its setting.” (Qur’an 50:39)
