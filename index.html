Welcome

Islam encourages us to remember Allah ﷻ throughout our daily lives. One of the most beautiful ways to do this is by reciting Duas (supplications). They are a shield of protection, a means of comfort, and a way to strengthen our connection with our Creator.

Why Recite Duas?

  • 🌅 Begin your day with Morning Duas for guidance and blessings.
  • 🌙 End your day with Evening Duas for peace and forgiveness.
  • 🛡️ Seek Allah’s shield with Protection Duas.

Hadith Reminder

“The supplication is the essence of worship.” – Prophet Muhammad ﷺ (Tirmidhi)
