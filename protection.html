🛡️ Protection Duas

Supplications to guard yourself and your loved ones.

Ayat al-Kursi

اللّه لا إله إلا هو الحي القيوم...

Reciting this verse after every prayer and before sleep grants Allah’s protection.

Three Quls

Recite Surah Al-Ikhlas, Surah Al-Falaq, and Surah An-Naas in the morning and evening three times for protection.

“Say: I seek refuge in the Lord of daybreak...” (Qur’an 113:1)
